Goodbye World

A program to greet the planet.

This page is an attempt to greet everyone goodbye on Earth using our various human languages. We are interested to see how long our list can become.

We will start with languages that the Acedu staff could offer without using Google.

  • English: Hello, world!
  • Yoruba: O da bo, agbaye!
  • Japanese: Soyonara, sekai!
  • French: au revoir, monde!
  • Kinyarwanda: Urabeho, isi!
